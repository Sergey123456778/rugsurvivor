💀 What's RugSurvivor?

RugSurvivor (RSV) is a meme token honoring those who endured rug pulls, scams, and liquidation. It’s a badge of pain — and glory.

🧶 What is a Rug Pull?

"Rug pull" is a crypto scam where project creators suddenly withdraw all funds and disappear, leaving investors with worthless tokens. Like pulling the rug from under your feet — fast and painful.

RugSurvivor exists to mock the chaos and honor those who still stand.
